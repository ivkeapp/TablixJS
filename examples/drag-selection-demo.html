You can now click and drag to select multiple rows in one smooth motion! This feature works alongside existing selection methods.

How to Use Drag Selection:

  • Click & Drag: Click on a row and drag to other rows to select a range
  • Combined Selection:
    • Use Ctrl+Click to add/remove individual rows
    • Use Shift+Click for keyboard-based range selection
    • Drag selection preserves existing selections
  • Single Mode: Drag selection is disabled (only available in multi mode)
  • Visual Feedback: Cursor changes to crosshair during drag operations
